Twin Cities Bicycle Fix-It Station Map

One day, I wondered whether or not there was a map where one can see where all the fix-it stations in the Twin Cities were. What if someone's bike broke down and they weren't anywhere near a bike shop? What if they were, but they didn't have time to work on their bike? So I biked around Minneapolis and sent Public Records Requests to every park, school, city, and county in the Twin Cities. I then used the data I got to make this map.

The map is still a work in progress because either I haven't gotten around to inspecting the fix-it stations that the entities have informed me of, or they haven't gotten back to me. If you notice anything off, send me an e-mail.
